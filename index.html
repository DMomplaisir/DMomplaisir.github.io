Dylan Momplaisir

Dylan Momplaisir is a high school junior at the HS of Economics and Finance, who is currently participating in the All Star Code Summer Intensive.

Prior to AllStarCode, he co-founded a startup called VOCAP with five other NYC students in a startup incubator called Generation Tech. VOCAP was a foriegn language learning application which allowed users to watch their favorite movies, or tv shows and learn a new language at the same time via subtitles, and consistent testing of new terms.This application won second place in the Generation Tech My City, My School Competition's Demo Day.

Aside from technology, he's a huge fan of the show House of Cards, and loves to help his community out either by interning at the Jewish Museum, or representing his classmates as a Class President
