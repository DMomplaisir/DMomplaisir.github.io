about me

I'm Dylan Momplaisir, a sophmore at Amherst College majoring in Architectural Studies, and Computer Science. I'm the Digital Director of the Amherst Student (the oldest school newspaper in the country), and UX/Pre-Sales Intern at Unqork. I've been doing front-end web development and Ui/UX design for about four years, and currently expanding into doing back-end development.

Being born and raised in NYC, I've been raised around a world of inequality, and that's motivated me to use tech for public good, supporting people in engaging with civil activism (Root), and learning more about their world (the Amherst Student). I'm interested in doing work that provides people opportunties to utilize resources eeasier, simplifying people's day to day work, and providing unique, interactive experienes which allow people to create more.
