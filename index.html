so, about me

There isn't an Apple Store in the Amazon, and you can't wait for Customer Service when you're trying to save lives. I believe that a combination of programming prowess, and understanding of user experience will allow us to build platforms for the next billion Internet users to speak their mind, build buisnesses, and live in a more interconnected world.

When I'm not blabbing about User Experience, I intern at the Jewish Museum, teaching the public about the arts (mostly children) via art projects, tours, and physical lesssons. I'm also Editor in Chief of a magazine called LitMag, a literary magazine with a fifteen person team. Half of the hours in my week go to LitMag because I feel a duty to show the literary and artistic talents of teens. Click here to view our most recent magazine.

Some odd hobbies are attempting to learn Arabic, being fluent in Haitian Creole, binge watching House of Cards, occasional acting, and loving jazz music.
